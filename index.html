About the Creator

Behind the name After Blink is a passionate self-taught animator who began his animation journey in 2024. Like many artists starting out, his earliest works were far from perfect. The drawings felt rough, the movement was stiff, and every project seemed to reveal another skill that needed improvement. Yet instead of giving up, he chose to keep creating.

What started as simple experiments gradually became a genuine passion. As his love for animation grew, so did his willingness to explore new techniques, tools, and creative challenges. Every finished scene became an opportunity to learn something new. Every mistake became a lesson. Every animation, no matter how small, pushed him one step closer to the stories he wanted to tell.

Inspired by anime, cinematic storytelling, and character-driven worlds, he continues to study the craft of animation while building his own unique style. From sketching keyframes and designing characters to experimenting with visual effects and storytelling, he enjoys every part of the creative process. His goal is not only to create entertaining animations but also to continuously evolve as an artist with each project he completes.

Today, After Blink focuses on creating original animated content, sharing creative experiments, and documenting his journey as he grows from a passionate beginner into a skilled storyteller. Whether it's action scenes, emotional moments, animation breakdowns, or behind-the-scenes progress updates, every piece of work represents another step forward.

This website serves as a home for his creations, ideas, and ongoing projects. Visitors can explore artwork, animations, development updates, and future releases while following the growth of a creator who believes that improvement comes from persistence, curiosity, and a genuine love for the craft.

The journey is still only beginning, and there is much more to come.
